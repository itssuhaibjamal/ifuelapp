What is iFuel App?

iFuel App connects fuel stations with their customers, and it also makes all the work of their employees much easier, for example, that the person filling the fuel easily use or store data using the iFuel App easily, that all the last steps can be easily accessed, that if needed, a customer's data can be accessed efficiently and quickly without spending any time , that the fuel station can easily import or store the new fuel that has arrived or landed, or the amount of fuel that they have sold in the last months or while using the App, they can also easily store their movements, for example, the amount of money that came in, or went out, and how they came in or went out of each with the information or explanation of where it went.
